Frequently Asked Questions

Products

Are all products 100% authentic?

+

Yes, all our cricket equipment is 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized dealers and manufacturers. We guarantee genuine products from brands like MRF, PUMA, SG, SS, Kookaburra, and more.

How do I choose the right cricket bat?

+

Choose based on your playing level and budget:

  • English Willow: Professional players, premium quality (₹15,000+)
  • Kashmir Willow: Amateur players, good quality (₹5,000-₹10,000)
  • Weight: 1100-1200g for adults, lighter for juniors

Check product descriptions for detailed specifications.

What is the warranty on cricket equipment?

+

Most products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty against manufacturing defects. Warranty does not cover normal wear and tear or damage from misuse. Please check individual product pages for specific warranty details.

Do you sell cricket balls for match play?

+

Yes, we stock premium leather cricket balls from Kookaburra, SF, and County suitable for match play. We also have practice tennis balls. All leather balls are available in red, white, and pink colors.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Products must be unused, in original packaging with all tags intact. Manufacturing defects are covered under warranty.

How do I return a product?

+

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact us at info@cricketpro.com with your Order ID
  2. Provide reason for return and product photos
  3. Our team will approve and arrange pickup
  4. Refund will be processed within 7-10 business days

How long does it take to get a refund?

+

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned product. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Can I exchange a product?

+

Yes, exchanges are possible subject to product availability. Contact us within 7 days of delivery to request an exchange. The product must be unused and in original condition.
